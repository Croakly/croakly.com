Our Mission

Croakly was founded by a team of educators, developers, and parents who believe that screen time can be positive, productive, and powerful. We were tired of seeing educational apps that were either boring or filled with distractions.

Our mission is simple: to create high-quality educational experiences that learners of all ages are excited to use. We combine proven teaching methods with top-tier game design to spark curiosity and build confidence in learners. Every app we make is a labor of love, crafted to be a delightful part of a learner's journey.
